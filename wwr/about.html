History

WildRiver Adventures was born in 2015 when three childhood friends—Jake, Emily, and Carlos—decided to turn their love for rivers and nature into a business. After years of rafting together through the wild rapids of the Colorado River, they knew they had something special to share.

Starting with just two inflatable rafts and a pickup truck, they offered weekend tours to locals and tourists looking for adrenaline and unforgettable scenery. Their passion, safety-first mindset, and personal touch quickly gained them loyal customers.

Over the years, WildRiver grew. Today, they offer multi-day rafting expeditions, team-building retreats, and eco-tourism experiences—all guided by certified professionals who share the founders' original love for the wild.

Their mission remains simple: Adventure with purpose, memories for life.
